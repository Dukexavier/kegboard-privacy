Keg Board — Privacy Policy

Effective: April 19, 2026

Keg Board is developed and published by Novarite Studios. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Keg Board stores all data locally on your device using browser localStorage. No personal data is transmitted to Novarite Studios or any third party by default.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable Cloud Sync, the app connects to your own Firebase Firestore project using credentials you provide (Project ID and API Key). Data synced includes board configuration, member names, keg information, and weigh-in history. Novarite Studios does not host, access, or control your Firebase project.

IoT Scale Data (Optional)

If you use the Auto-Scale feature with an ESP32 device, weight readings are sent to your own Firebase Firestore project. No scale data passes through Novarite Studios servers.

Photos and Images

Photos uploaded (member photos, brewery logos, can images, keg fridge photos) are stored locally on your device as base64 data within localStorage. When Cloud Sync is enabled, photos are not synced to Firestore to stay within document size limits.

Advertising

The free version of Keg Board may display ads served by third-party ad networks. These networks may collect device identifiers and usage data per their own privacy policies. The Pro version is ad-free.

Analytics

Keg Board does not include analytics SDKs or tracking code. No usage data is collected by Novarite Studios.

Children

Keg Board is intended for users of legal drinking age. The app is not directed at children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
